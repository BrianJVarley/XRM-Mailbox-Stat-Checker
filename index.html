Welcome to XRM Mailbox Diagnostic Tool.

The XRM Mailbox Stat Checker is a diagnostic tool that checks the status of Microsoft Dynamics CRM mailboxes that are in "pending send status".

Mailbox Stat Checker also enables and approves emails and mailboxes at the press of a button.

Overview

Why are emails in pending send status?

This is a common question in Dynamics CRM and means the email hasn’t been processed yet by the email router or it was processed and there was an erorr .In most cases this is due to one of two missing settings on the user or queue’s email configurations in CRM:

1.Email address isn’t “Approved” – This setting approves the selected email to send emails from Dynamics.

2.Mailbox isn’t “Tested and Enabled” – Testing and enabling a mailbox does slightly more than the approve email setting and only applies to emails with server-side synchronization. It also checks the following details:

◙The mailbox email address is correct. ◙The connection to the email server is working. ◙You have the right privileges to access the mailbox.

Luckily I’ve created a tool to do just that for queue and user’s email addresses.

Note that to do either of these configuration changes you need to be signed into the tool as a System Admin or the owner of the mailbox.

After approving or enabling the email using the tool, resend the email in CRM wait 10 minutes for the email router to process the email. Then check the status of the pending send email. You should now see either a status of “sending” or “sent”.
